Play Baby Shark with a keyboard!

When you press PLAY, baby sharkes will fall down.

Try to catch them when they reach the pink circles by pressing the corresponding buttons while listening to the background music carefully.

The more accurate you play the notes and the longer combos you achieve, the more you will score!

This game is optimal to play in 1920 x 1080.
