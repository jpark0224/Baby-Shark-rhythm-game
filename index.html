Play Baby Shark with a keyboard!

Press PLAY to make baby sharks fall down and start a song.

Try to catch them when they reach the pink circles.

You can do this by pressing the corresponding buttons while listening to the song carefully.

The more accurate you play the notes and the longer combos you achieve, the more you will score!

A browser height over 900px is recommended for this game.
